  • Relaxation Hub

It's all fine to feel burn out. Sometime despite putting all our effots, we still cannot give our best. We fell exhaused and our brain cannot further process any information. If you feel something like this, Don't wory its fine everybody feels it. You just need to give some rest to your brain. So, here are some movies, songs, series, and podcast I recommend you to watch if you feel down. It might help you recharge and start fresh.

Relaxation Hub

Take a break and immerse yourself in soothing entertainment to refresh your mind and soul.

Movies

Escape into captivating stories and stunning visuals that challenge your mind.

  • Interstellar - A visually spectacular journey through space, time, and human emotion. Explore the mysteries of the universe and the bond between a father and his daughter.
  • Avatar - An epic tale set on the alien world of Pandora, filled with breathtaking visuals and an exploration of environmentalism, culture, and personal growth.
  • The Shawshank Redemption - A deeply moving story of friendship, hope, and perseverance within the confines of a prison.
  • A Beautiful Mind - The inspiring true story of mathematician John Nash, who battles with schizophrenia while striving to solve an incredible mathematical problem.
  • Up - A heartwarming tale about an elderly man who goes on an adventure to fulfill his deceased wife's dream, teaching us about love and letting go.
  • Good Will Hunting - A moving story about a janitor with a genius-level intellect who struggles to accept help and embrace his potential.
  • The Pursuit of Happyness - A powerful and uplifting story of resilience, determination, and love as a man fights for a better life for his son.
  • Forrest Gump - A timeless classic about an esxtraordinary man living through extraordinary events, filled with life lessons and inspiration.
  • The Secret Life of Walter Mitty - A journey of self-discovery and adventure as Walter Mitty steps out of his mundane life and embarks on a global quest.
  • Amélie - A whimsical French tale about a shy young woman who secretly helps those around her while seeking love and happiness in her own life.

Series

Binge-worthy shows that combine entertainment with meaningful life lessons.

  • Friends - A hilarious and heartwarming sitcom about a group of friends navigating life in New York City.
  • Brooklyn Nine-Nine - A comedy series set in a quirky NYPD precinct that balances humor with a touch of mystery.
  • Schitt’s Creek - A family loses their fortune and moves to a small town, where they learn to reconnect with each other and their roots.
  • Queer Eye - A heartwarming reality show where five experts transform the lives of individuals through style, grooming, culture, and more.
  • The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel - A 1950s housewife pursues a career in stand-up comedy after her husband leaves her, defying societal expectations.
  • Ted Lasso - An American football coach becomes the head coach of a British soccer team, winning over hearts with his optimism and kindness.
  • Parks and Recreation - A comedic take on local government with an ensemble cast navigating the ups and downs of small-town politics.
  • Stranger Things - A group of kids in a small town uncover dark secrets while trying to find their missing friend, blending sci-fi and nostalgia.
  • The Good Place - A unique afterlife comedy where a woman learns what it means to be a good person while in "the Good Place."
  • The Office - A mockumentary-style sitcom about the daily lives of employees at a mundane office, with dry humor and quirky characters.

Podcasts

Thoughtful conversations to relax and reflect on life, mindfulness, and the world around us.

  • The Daily - Insightful news, calmly told, to help you stay informed and unwind.
    Listen on Spotify
  • On Being - Deep, soothing discussions on spirituality, humanity, and life.
    Listen on Spotify
  • Calm Sage - Mindfulness and peace for navigating the stresses of everyday life.
    Listen on Spotify
  • TED Talks Daily - Thought-provoking and inspiring talks on topics ranging from technology to personal growth.
    Listen on Spotify
  • The Tim Ferriss Show - Interviews with top performers to discuss their routines, strategies, and mindset.
    Listen on Spotify

Other Recommendations:

  • Listen to songs that motivate and excite you
  • Take a walk, play with friends, and talk to your family
  • Travel, visit somewhere peaceful, and help somebody
  • Avoid using your phone for a while
